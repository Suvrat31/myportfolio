More About Me

A Computer Science student with 2+ years of software development experience in full stack development, object oriented programming and deploying microservices to the cloud in an Agile environment.

Howdy!

I am a motivated Computer Science enthusiast currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at Arizona State University. Prior to this, I completed a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies in India, which solidified my understanding of core computer science concepts. During my tenure at Cognizant, India, I transitioned from an intern to a pivotal role as a Software Engineer, contributing significantly to optimizing a cloud migration product. My responsibilities encompassed full-cycle development, from feature implementation to bug fixes, feasibility assessments, and security enhancements.

Additionally, I hold certifications such as AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner and received the Google India Challenge Scholarship. Volunteering for visually impaired resume-building exercises and participating as a finalist in the Mobile Innovation Challenge Hackathon further underscore my commitment beyond conventional responsibilities.
